{ project overview }

Embracing the rich tapestry of flavors that Indian cuisine offers, the Indian Food Tracker project is a personal endeavor aimed at addressing the challenge of finding a specialized tool to track and explore the diverse world of Indian culinary delights. Fueled by a passion for Indian food and a desire for a more tailored experience, this project is designed to be a dedicated food tracker for my friends and family.

Unlike generic food tracking apps, the Indian Food Tracker is tailored to the specific requirements of tracking Indian meals, ensuring a more accurate and personalized tracking experience. Furthermore, By offering detailed insights into the nutritional content of Indian ingredients and dishes, I can make informed decisions about my dietary choices, promoting a healthier lifestyle.

This was my first ever Python project :)
